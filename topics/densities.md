# Densities

Cross-session extracts on the density/octave structure of consciousness evolution. Ra describes eight densities (or "octaves") through which all consciousness evolves toward reunification with the Infinite Creator.

## Overview

Density is Ra's term for what might be called a "dimension" or "plane" — a level of vibration and awareness. There are eight densities in one octave of creation, with the eighth density becoming the first density of the next octave.

> *"The system of densities is a system of growing love/light and light/love. Each density has a certain frequency of vibration."* — Ra

---

## First Density: Awareness (Elements)

The foundation — the consciousness of earth, water, fire, and air. Minerals and elements becoming aware of existence.

> *Session 13:* "The first density is the density of awareness. The consciousness of earth, water, fire, and air — that which you call the elements — is the first density."

**Duration:** Approximately 2 billion years for planetary evolution  
**Lesson:** Simple awareness; being

---

## Second Density: Growth (Plants and Animals)

Biological life — plants, animals — developing individuation and will.

> *Session 13:* "The second density is the density of the higher plant life and animal life which exists without the upward drive towards the Creator, but which exists and is aware of other-selves."

> *Session 19:* "The second density strives towards the third density which is the density of self-consciousness, or self-awareness."

**Duration:** Approximately 4.6 billion years on Earth  
**Lesson:** Growth, individuation, the desire to evolve

---

## Third Density: Choice (Humanity)

Self-consciousness, free will, and the great choice: Service-to-Others (positive) or Service-to-Self (negative). This is the "density of choice" and the density humans currently inhabit.

> *Session 16:* "The third density is the density of self-consciousness, or self-awareness. It is the density of choice. The choice is: shall the entity grow in love and light serving others, or shall the entity grow serving only itself?"

> *Session 40:* "In the third density, the veil of forgetting is intact. The entity does not remember its origin or its destiny. This is necessary for the choice to be made in total freedom."

**Duration:** 75,000 years (three 25,000-year cycles)  
**Lesson:** The choice of polarity — love of self or love of other  
**Graduation requirement:** 51% service-to-others OR 95% service-to-self

---

## Fourth Density: Love/Understanding

The density of love and compassion. Positive fourth density is characterized by social memory complexes — groups of entities whose minds and memories are shared.

> *Session 28:* "The fourth density is a vibrational complex which begins to be, as you would say, in your near future. In this density there is a more transparent veil between the conscious and unconscious mind."

> *Session 41:* "The fourth density is one of immense love and understanding. The social memory complex begins to form. Entities share their experiences, their joys, and their sorrows."

**Duration:** Approximately 30 million years  
**Lesson:** Absolute compassion; understanding others as self

---

## Fifth Density: Wisdom/Light

The density of wisdom. Entities here have mastered love and now develop wisdom — the understanding of how love operates.

> *Session 43:* "The fifth density is the density of wisdom. It is possible to deduce that there are entities in this density who are of great wisdom. However, wisdom without love is somewhat like light without heat — it illuminates but does not warm."

**Duration:** Approximately 75 million years  
**Lesson:** Wisdom; the intelligent application of love

---

## Sixth Density: Unity of Love and Wisdom

Love and wisdom are reunified. Entities here have both mastered compassion and wisdom and begin to approach unity consciousness.

> *Session 34:* "The sixth density is the density of unity. At this point the positive and negative paths merge, for all have learned that love and wisdom must be unified."

Ra itself is a sixth-density social memory complex.

**Duration:** Approximately 150 million years  
**Lesson:** The unification of love and wisdom

---

## Seventh Density: Gateway to the Infinite

The density of foreverness — entities approach the gateway back to the Infinite Creator. Individual identity begins to dissolve.

> *Session 16:* "The seventh density is the density of completion, the density of foreverness. The entity moves toward the Creator in complete unity and absolute love."

**Duration:** Immeasurable  
**Lesson:** Complete surrender to the Creator

---

## Eighth Density: The Octave / Return to Unity

The eighth density is simultaneously the last density and the first density of the next octave. All experience is gathered and returned to the Infinite Creator, which then begins again.

> *Session 28:* "The eighth density is the octave which moves into a mystery we do not plumb. It is the gateway to another octave of densities."
