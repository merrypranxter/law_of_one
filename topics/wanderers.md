# Wanderers

Wanderers are entities from higher densities (typically fourth, fifth, or sixth) who choose to incarnate in third density — specifically on Earth — to assist with the Harvest and the spiritual evolution of the planet.

## What Is a Wanderer?

A Wanderer is an entity who has already graduated from third density and progressed to a higher density, but who has volunteered to return to third-density incarnation to serve others.

> *Session 12:* "The term 'Wanderer' is used to describe those entities who have left their home density and incarnated in third density in order to serve. They come from fourth, fifth, and sixth densities."

> *Session 65:* "Wanderers are those who have answered the call. The call is not a verbal call. It is the call of the need of your peoples. When a planet is in the difficulties that yours is in, entities from higher densities hear this call and volunteer to incarnate."

---

## Why Wanderers Come to Earth

Wanderers incarnate for several primary reasons:
1. **To share love and light** — their very vibration raises the collective consciousness
2. **To serve as channels** — many facilitate healing, teaching, and channeling
3. **To anchor higher-density energies** — their presence helps stabilize the vibratory shift
4. **Karmic service** — some have karmic connections to Earth

> *Session 17:* "The purpose of the Wanderer is to serve. Simply by being here, the Wanderer offers the light of a higher density. This is a service that is not always conscious, yet it is real."

---

## The Challenge of Wanderers

Wanderers face a significant challenge: **the veil of forgetting**. When they incarnate in third density, they forget their true nature and higher-density origins. This is necessary for free will to be preserved.

> *Session 12:* "The Wanderer forgets, upon incarnation, its identity. This is part of the Law of Confusion — the law of free will. The Wanderer must rediscover its nature through the veil."

> *Session 36:* "Many Wanderers find third density very difficult. They sense that they are different. They may feel alienated from the culture around them. They may yearn for something they cannot name. This yearning is the memory of home."

---

## Signs and Characteristics

Ra describes common characteristics of Wanderers:

- A sense of not belonging on Earth or feeling "alien"
- Unusual sensitivity — to light, sound, emotion
- Strong orientation toward service and healing
- Curiosity about metaphysical and spiritual matters
- Memory or longing for a "home" that isn't Earth
- Difficulty with the harshness and confusion of third density

> *Session 46:* "The Wanderer often finds the density of choice uncomfortable. The catalyst of third density — conflict, suffering, confusion — is jarring to an entity that has known fourth-density love or fifth-density wisdom. Yet this discomfort is itself a form of service, for the Wanderer's sensitivity is a finely tuned instrument."

---

## Number of Wanderers on Earth

Ra gives estimates of the number of Wanderers incarnated on Earth:

> *Session 12:* "We estimate the number of Wanderers on your planet at this time to be approximately sixty-five million."

> *Session 63:* "The number of Wanderers has been increasing as the time of harvest approaches. This is in response to the increasing need."

---

## The Risk of Wanderers

Wanderers risk what Ra calls "the forgetting" going too deep — becoming lost in third density and failing to awaken or complete their service. They also risk accumulating negative karma.

> *Session 12:* "The risk of the Wanderer is that the forgetting may be so complete that the entity becomes confused about its nature and does not awaken. However, the love of the Creator is such that this risk, though real, is not as great as it might seem. The Wanderer has a built-in homing signal."
