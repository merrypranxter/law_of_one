# The Harvest

The Harvest is one of the central concepts of the Law of One. It refers to the graduation of third-density consciousness to fourth density — a cyclical event that occurs approximately every 75,000 years.

## What Is the Harvest?

At the end of each 25,000-year minor cycle, and especially at the end of three such cycles (75,000 years), there is an opportunity for third-density entities to graduate — or be "harvested" — into fourth density. Those who have sufficiently polarized (either service-to-others or service-to-self) graduate. Those who have not must repeat third density elsewhere.

> *Session 17:* "The harvest is a time when the vibratory rate of this planet changes from what you call third density to fourth density. Entities who have vibrated sufficiently in love and light, or in love of self, are ready to move to the next density of experience."

> *Session 22:* "At each major cycle's end, those who have learned the lessons of their particular density are harvested. Those who have not learned continue in a third density on another planet or in another version of this one."

---

## The Current Harvest — Earth's Transition

Earth is currently in the process of transitioning from third density to fourth density. This is described as a particularly significant event because it is occurring alongside a broader shift in the vibratory quality of the galaxy.

> *Session 40:* "Your planet is at this time in the process of graduating from third to fourth density. This process was begun in what you call the nineteen-sixties."

> *Session 63:* "The harvest of your planet is ongoing. It is not a single moment in time. Many entities have already been harvested; others will be harvested as the process continues."

---

## Graduation Requirements

### Positive Path (Service-to-Others)
Entities must be oriented at least **51% service-to-others** to graduate on the positive path.

> *Session 17:* "The entity must be more than fifty percent oriented to the service of others. This is the minimum for harvest into the positive fourth density."

### Negative Path (Service-to-Self)
Entities must be oriented at least **95% service-to-self** to graduate on the negative path.

> *Session 17:* "The service-to-self path requires a much more rigorous orientation — ninety-five percent service to self."

---

## Those Who Do Not Graduate

Entities who have not chosen a polarity, or who remain in the 50/50 confusion of mixed orientation, are not harvested. They continue third-density experience, typically on another third-density planet.

> *Session 25:* "Those who do not graduate continue their lessons in third density. There is no punishment in this. The Creator loves all Its parts equally. The entity simply continues to grow."

---

## The Three Harvest Cycles

Earth's 75,000-year major cycle consists of three 25,000-year minor cycles. At the end of each minor cycle, a harvest was possible:
- ~25,000 years ago: First minor harvest
- ~50,000 years ago: Second minor harvest  
- ~75,000 years ago (approximately now): The major harvest
