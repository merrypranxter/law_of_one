# Session 1
**Date:** January 15, 1981  
**Questioner:** Don Elkins  
**Channeled through:** Carla L. Rueckert  
**Topics:** `#introduction` `#confederation` `#contact-mechanics` `#law-of-one` `#free-will`

---

## 1.0

**Ra:** I am Ra. I have not spoken through this instrument before. We had to wait until she was precisely tuned, as we send a narrow-band vibration. We greet you in the love and in the light of our Infinite Creator.

We have watched your group. We have been called to your group, for you have a need for the diversity of experiences in channeling which go with a more intensive, or as you might call it, advanced approach to the system of studying the patterns of the illusions of your body, your mind, and your spirit, which you call seeking the truth. We hope to offer you a somewhat different slant upon the information which is always and ever the same.

The Confederation of Planets in the Service of the Infinite Creator has only one important statement. That statement, my friends, as you know, is "All things, beings, and thoughts are one."

We will begin by matching our instruments. It will take three sessions for your instrument to become correctly tuned in order to prevent overloading of this instrument. We will cease speaking and you may question us.

---

## 1.1

**Questioner:** It seems members of the Confederation have a specific purpose that they are attempting to carry out. Could you tell us something about this purpose?

**Ra:** I am Ra. We communicate now. We, too, have our place. We are not those of the Love or of the Light. We are those who are of the Law of One. In the beginning of being which is not a beginning, in the creation which is formed nothingness, there is what you call Love. This Love becomes Light. This Light then becomes the many: your peoples, the peoples of other planets, those in other dimensions, those who have no name. The creation, then, is infinite in its possibilities and is unified by the Love/Light which permeates all things.

We serve by communicating this basic Law of One to those who are ready to hear. We are not evangelists. We do not knock upon doors. We are called, and we respond. Our purpose is simply to share what we know so that those who seek may find.

---

## 1.2

**Questioner:** How do you refer to yourself?

**Ra:** I am Ra. This is difficult. There is no precise word for this concept. "Ra" is a sound complex that your peoples have used, and we accept this. We are those of the Ra social memory complex who have been called by this group.

---

## 1.3

**Questioner:** Where are you from?

**Ra:** I am Ra. I am from the vibratory level of your call. This is difficult to explain in your terms. We are from the density of understanding which you might call the sixth density. However, we have no physical location in the sense that your peoples understand location. We are from the density of the Confederation, which means that we are from the Love/Light, the distorted Love/Light of the Confederation.

More specifically, we were those of the planet which you call Venus. We are a social memory complex which has evolved through your own solar system. We chose this system as our home for reasons which will become clear as we speak of the Law of One.

---

## 1.4

**Questioner:** I'm trying to understand how you are able to speak to us. Can you tell me something about that?

**Ra:** I am Ra. I am speaking to you through this instrument. The channel is a direct line between myself and the instrument. There is no other way to speak to you at this time. We send a narrow-band vibration and the instrument receives it, and through the process which your peoples call channeling, the message is transmitted to the recording device.

This instrument is what you might call a natural channel. She has the ability to receive and transmit the vibrations which we send. We have been in contact with this instrument for many of your years and have been waiting for the precise moment when the contact could be made.

---

## 1.5

**Questioner:** Can you tell me your understanding of what happens after we die?

**Ra:** I am Ra. I am glad you asked this question, for it is fundamental to an understanding of the Law of One.

When the physical body ceases to function, what you call the soul or spirit continues. It continues in a state which you might call an in-between state, a state of review. In this state, the entity reviews the life just completed and makes choices about the next experience.

The entity then incarnates again, or moves on to a higher density. The choice depends upon the level of evolution of the consciousness. If the entity has learned the lessons of the third density — primarily the lessons of love and the choosing of polarity — the entity may then graduate to the fourth density.

The fourth density is a density of love and understanding, where the entity continues to grow in love. It is a very beautiful state of being.

---

## 1.6

**Questioner:** What is the significance of the Law of One?

**Ra:** I am Ra. The Law of One, though beyond the limitations of name, as you call vibratory sound complexes, may be approximated by stating that all things are one, that there is no polarity, no right or wrong, no disharmony, but only identity. All is one, and that one is love/light, light/love, the Infinite Creator.

This is the Law of One. It is not a law in the sense of your peoples' understanding of laws. It is a statement of the nature of the universe. All things are one Creator. There is no separation. There is no "other." There is only the One Infinite Creator experiencing Itself through the infinite possibilities of creation.

---

## 1.7

**Questioner:** Thank you very much. I look forward to talking with you again.

**Ra:** I am Ra. I am pleased to have spoken with this group. I leave you in the love and the light of the One Infinite Creator. Go forth, therefore, rejoicing in the power and in the peace of the One Infinite Creator. Adonai.
