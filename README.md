# The Law of One — Repository

> *"I am Ra. I greet you in the love and in the light of the one infinite Creator."*

## What Is the Law of One?

The Law of One is a series of 106 channeled sessions recorded between 1981 and 1984. A social memory complex calling itself **Ra** communicated through **Carla L. Rueckert**, with questions from **Don Elkins** and notes by **Jim McCarty**. The material covers cosmology, consciousness evolution, densities of existence, free will, service-to-others vs. service-to-self polarity, and humanity's spiritual history.

The sessions were published as five books by L/L Research and are freely available at [lawofone.info](https://www.lawofone.info).

## Key Concepts at a Glance

- **Densities**: 8 levels of consciousness (1=elements, 2=growth, 3=choice, 4=love, 5=wisdom, 6=unity, 7=gateway, 8=octave)
- **The Harvest**: Earth's current transition from 3rd to 4th density (approximately 75,000-year cycle)
- **Polarity**: Service-to-Others (positive) vs. Service-to-Self (negative) — the fundamental choice of 3rd density
- **The Confederation**: Group of positive entities guiding galactic evolution
- **The Orion Group**: Negative polarity entities opposing the Confederation
- **Free Will (The Law of Confusion)**: The primary law — no entity may infringe upon another's free will
